A Little More About Us

HERO's goals currently encompass the combined tasks of locomotion and manipulation, dubbed: "loco-manipulation". Loco-manipulation for legged robots is one of the hardest problems in robotics. We intend to use our in-house switched system solver (Galileo) to build a multi-modal tree in real-time which will allow us to solve both the optimal trajectory (a continuous problem) and the optimal contact sequence (a discrete problem) simultaneously. This approach was inspired by the minds at ETH-Zurich in their Versatile Multi-Contact Planning and Control for Legged Loco-Manipulation publication.

HERO is composed of a team of WPI students invested in furthering the field of Legged Robotics. We are constantly looking for sponsors and recruiting new students to continue our work! We hope that the BiQu and HURON projects continue to stand tall, as a testemant to WPI's dedication to research and innovation in Legged Robotics. Please reach out to us at gr-biqu24@wpi.edu if you are interested.
