About

I am an Associate Teaching Professor in the Robotics Engineering Department at Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI), with a joint appointment in Mechanical Engineering. I lead the Autonomous Loco-Manipulation Systems (ALMaS) Group.

My research focuses on legged robotics, construction robotics, mobile manipulation, robotic 3D printing, and autonomous robotic systems for construction and manufacturing applications. I am also interested in digital twins and digital manufacturing, especially where physical robotic systems connect with data-driven models and digital workflows. My teaching focuses on robotics, dynamics, and control, with an emphasis on project-based learning and the tight integration of theory, experimentation, and real robotic systems.
